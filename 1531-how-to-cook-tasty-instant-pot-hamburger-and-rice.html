Ingredients of Instant Pot Hamburger and Rice

  1. You need 1 pound of ground beef (2 pounds).
  2. You need 1/2 of onion diced (1).
  3. It's 1 can of cream of mushroom soup (2).
  4. Prepare 2 1/2 cups of chicken broth (3 1/2).
  5. It's 1 cup of rice (2).
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  7. Prepare 2 stalks of celery diced (celery seeds as alternative (3).

Instant Pot Hamburger and Rice instructions

  1. Sauté hamburger, onion and celery in instant pot and drain grease.
  2. Use the ingredients in parentheses to double.
  3. Combine chicken broth and can of cream of mushroom soup until smooth. Pour mixture over the hamburger mixture.
  4. Sprinkle rice over top of all ingredients and press it into the liquid.
  5. Set instant Pot on manual for 10 minutes. When done quick release. Stir and let set for about 5 minutes then serve.
