Ingredients of Taiwanese minced pork rice bowl

  1. Prepare 1 lb of gound pork.
  2. It's 4 of garlic cloves.
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon of dry shallots.
  4. You need 2 tablespoons of oyster sauce.
  5. It's 3 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  6. You need 1 tablespoon of brown sugar (optional).
  7. It's 1/4 cups of rice wine (mijiu).
  8. You need 1 teaspoon of white pepper powder.
  9. It's 1 teaspoon of five spice powder.
  10. You need 3/4 cups of hot water.

Taiwanese minced pork rice bowl instructions

  1. Pan-fry ground pork until the fat is rendered. This takes about 10 minutes. Now the temperature is high enough for the next step..
  2. Pan-fry sliced garlic and dry shallot with the meat and the pork fat. You should be able to smell the garlic in 1-2 minutes..
  3. Stir while seasoning: add oyster sauce, soy sauce, and brown sugar (optional)..
  4. Add rice wine and wait until boiled. Add white pepper and five spice, mix well..
  5. Finally, add 3/4 cups of hot water, turn down the heat and stew for 30 minutes..
