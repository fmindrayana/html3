Chicken Paws Korean Style Instant Pot IP instructions

  1. What I found at my local grocery.
  2. If required clean your chicken feet, and optionally clip the toe nails, place in a bowl.
  3. Add marinade ingredients to bowl, mix well. Let sit for 30 minutes to 2 hours, stir occasionally..
  4. Add oil to IP liner, put IP on saute.
  5. Put chicken feet in liner right away, to cold oil so they don't pop and burn you, save remaining marinade.
  6. Add left over marinade to smaller bowl. Add rest of sauce ingredients, Stir..
  7. Stir chicken feet often until a little browned. Add chicken broth and deglaze liner.
  8. Add sauce, stir it up.
  9. Set IP to pressure cook for 20 minutes.
  10. Allow to natural pressure release 10 minutes, quick release the remaining pressure.
  11. Now you can cook the sauce until thick, on saute stirring frequently, or remove and eat immediately, like I do.
  12. Garnish with green onions and/or sesame seeds..
