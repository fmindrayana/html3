Spaghetti carbonara (5 ingredient ) step by step

  1. Cook spaghetti . While spaghetti is cooking dice up your bacon..
  2. Cook bacon about 6 to 8 minutes. Crispy is what your looking for. Don't drain the bacon grease..
  3. While bacon is cooking whisk up eggs and grated parmesan.
  4. Add garlic to bacon . Cook a minute . Pasta should be done at this time . Keep some of the pasta water, at least a cup. Drain pasta..
  5. Turn pan to low temp.Add spaghetti and egg mixture. Fold in to mix. Add tablespoon at a time reserved pasta water . Cook a couple of minutes to desired consistency..
  6. I served it with parsley and extra shredded parmesan..
