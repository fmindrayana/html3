Rice pancakes (sinasir) step by step

  1. Soak rice for 4-6hrs or over night.
  2. Wash your rice and remove it from water.
  3. Use grinding machine to grind the rice smoothly.
  4. Pour the fresh milk in the batter and mix well.
  5. Cover the batter and keep it in warm place to rise for at least 3-4hrs.
  6. Put your baking powder, salt and (sugar if you like) and mix again.
  7. Put frypan on low heat and start your sinasir usin a teaspoon of oil.
  8. Pour the mixture on the heated oil.
  9. Cover the pan for the sinasir to cook.
