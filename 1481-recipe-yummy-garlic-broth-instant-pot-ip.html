Garlic broth instant pot ip instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in instant pot. Put on lid and cook at high pressure for an hour. Then allow a natural release for 1/2 an hour. Release rest of pressure..
  2. In a large bowl (able to hold more than 8 cups liquid) place a strainer and empty contents into strainer..
  3. Remove 6 cloves of garlic. Careful it will be hot..
  4. Using the back of a ladle, press all the liquid out of contents on the strainer. This will take some time. But you should get 8 cups total with enough work..
  5. Squeeze garlic out of skins that was set aside and whisk into the broth..
  6. Enjoy!!.
  7. Side note: this broth settles very quickly. So if looking for storing it or freezing it (good for 6 months frozen) make sure you give a good stir before separating it..
