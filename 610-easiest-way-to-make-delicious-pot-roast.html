Ingredients of Pot Roast

  1. It's 3-4 pound of beef roast.
  2. You need 2 lbs of baby carrots.
  3. You need 6 of large potatoes.
  4. It's 2 tablespoons of worchestire sauce.
  5. Prepare of RUB - I tbsp each salt, pepper, onion powder, garlic powder.
  6. It's 1 packet of beef stew seasoning (any brand).
  7. You need 1 cup of water.

Simply add all the ingredients to a slow cooker or Crock Pot, and you'll have dinner ready when you get home from work. Pot Roast instructions

  1. Mix ingredients for rub. Use 3/4 of it to coat all sides of roast..
  2. Heat skillet on high. Add worcestershire sauce. Sear each side of the roast in a very hot pan..
  3. Place roast in center of roasting pan. Cut potatoes into 4s. Place potatoes and carrots around roast. Sprinkle the reminder of the rub over meat, potatoes and carrots..
  4. Mix beef stew packet with 1/2 cup water. It will be thick! Drizzle it all over the meat, potatoes and carrots..
  5. Cover and bake at 250 for 2 1/2 hours. After the first hour add a 1/2 cup of water. Baste every half hour..
  6. After finished cooking let sit for 15 minutes. Slice meat..
  7. Serve with the potatoes and carrots. Broth will be thick and rich! Enjoy!.
