Ingredients of Microwave Brownies

  1. It's 3/4 cup of self-raising floor.
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup of unsweetened cocoa powder.
  3. You need 1 tsp of instant coffee.
  4. You need 1/4 tsp of salt.
  5. It's 2 of eggs.
  6. It's 1 cup of sugar.
  7. You need 3/4 cup of vegetable oil.
  8. It's 1 tsp of vanilla essence.
  9. Prepare 150 g of semi-sweet chocolate, chopped.

Microwave Brownies instructions

  1. Combine the first 4 ingredients together in a bowl, making sure no lumps are found..
  2. Then combine the next 4 ingredients in a separate bowl mixing it thoroughly..
  3. Carefully fold the dry batter into the second bowl adding gradually while mixing. Finally, add the chopped chocolate and mix it well..
  4. Line a microwave-safe dish with baking paper and grease it using butter or oil. Pour the batter and spreading it well in the dish..
  5. Microwave for 3-4 minutes. Enjoy!!.
