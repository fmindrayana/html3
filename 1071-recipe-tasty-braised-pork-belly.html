卤三层肉 Braised Pork Belly step by step

  1. 把三层肉，马铃薯和红萝卜切小块。放入压力锅 Cut pork belly, potato and carrot into small pieces. Put into pressure cooker..
  2. 加入蒜头 Add garlic.
  3. 加入一汤匙酱清，一汤匙酱油，少许胡椒粉。煮15分钟。 Add 1 tablespoon of light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, some pepper powder. Cook for 15mins..
