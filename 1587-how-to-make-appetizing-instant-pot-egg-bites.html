Instant pot egg bites instructions

  1. Above ingredients are the base recipe..
  2. Combo ideas: Smoked salmon & dill; ham & cheese; bacon & cheese; salsa & cheese; broccoli & cheese, etc....
  3. Place a trivet in the bottom of the Instant pot, then 5 silicone cups. Spray the inside of each cup with pam..
  4. Next add one egg per cup, breaking the yolk carefully..
  5. Add in a small amount of your choice of ingredients to each cup. Ie, a pinch of shredded cheese and 1" piece of bacon..
  6. Place a tall trivet in the pot..
  7. Repeat steps 3, 4, & 5.
  8. Close the lid, and place the vent to seal..
  9. Press manual and adjust the time to 6 minutes.
  10. When finished, quick release the pressure..
  11. Carefully remove the eggs cups and place on a cooling rack. Carefully dump or dab any water off the tops of the bottom eggs..
  12. Allow the eggs to cool for 5 minutes, then remove them from the cups..
  13. Enjoy now, or bag for the week..
