Ingredients of Pork binagoongan

  1. Prepare 2 lbs of pork shoulder (large cubes).
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp of shrimp paste.
  3. Prepare 3 cloves of garlic (crushed).
  4. Prepare 1 of small onion (sliced).
  5. You need 1 1/2 cup of water.
  6. It's 2 tbsp of vinegar.
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp of brown sugar.
  8. It's 2 of chili peppers (chopped) *optional.
  9. Prepare 1 of medium size tomato.

You may adjust the taste as preferred. It is an easy yet delicious dish. It commonly tastes salty as a result of its shrimp paste ingredient. This is a traditional Filipino food, you've got to try this, it's yummy~.

Pork binagoongan step by step

  1. Sauté pork cubes on high heat until browned and fat is rendered..
  2. Remove excess oil and add crushed garlic, until browned, then onion and tomato..
  3. Add shrimp paste and chili and mix..
  4. Pour water and vinegar then bring to a boil..
  5. Continue simmering until sauce is reduced..
  6. Garnish with chopped onion leeks. Serve hot.
