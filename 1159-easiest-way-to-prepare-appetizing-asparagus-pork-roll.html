Asparagus pork roll step by step

  1. Peel the skin of the asparagus with a peeler, and then cut it into 3..
  2. Roll the pork around the asparagus very tightly. If one roll is not enough, roll two. Need to use super thin pork..
  3. Fry the pork roll with medium heat. No oil needed..
  4. Put on the lid and wait until the pork roll surface gets slightly burned. Then flip over the roll..
  5. Add salt, pepper and white wine (or sake). Take off the lid and fry with strong heat. Add a bit of soy sauce in the end..
