Ingredients of Teekhi beef karai😋😋

  1. Prepare 1 kg of beef.
  2. You need 1.5 ltr of water.
  3. Prepare 1 tbs of salt.
  4. You need 2 tbs of black pepper.
  5. You need 1 tbs of peprica powder.
  6. You need 1 tbs of coriander powder.
  7. Prepare 3 of green chilli.
  8. You need 3 of tomatto.
  9. You need 200 gms of yoghurt.
  10. Prepare of green coriander.
  11. You need 1 cup of oil.
  12. You need 2 of onion.
  13. It's 2 tbs of ginger garlic paste.

Teekhi beef karai😋😋 step by step

  1. Take a pan add water and beef and boil until soft and cooked..
  2. Take a pan.add oil when oil is hot then add onion cook till brown in colour..
  3. Add beef and ginger garlic paste..
  4. Add salt peprica powder black pepper and tomatoo and coriander powder. then cook for 10 minute over low flame..
  5. Add yoghurt and cook over low flame for 10 minute..
  6. Garnish with finely chopped green chilli and coriander..
  7. Ready to serve it.😊😊.
