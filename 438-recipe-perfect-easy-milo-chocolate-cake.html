Easy milo chocolate cake instructions

  1. Add 1 tablespoon of hot water into the Milo powder and mix it in a bowl.
  2. Beat 1 egg.
  3. Add sugar into the egg.
  4. Whisk well..
  5. Add all-purpose flour into the mixture.
  6. Mix well with a pair of chopsticks if you don't have a whisk.
  7. Add melted butter and mix well..
  8. Grease the mold with butter and pour the cake batter into the mold.
  9. Steam it in a steamer with the highest heat for 8 minutes (the time depends on the size of your mold).
  10. Done!.
