Ingredients of Salty crunchy snack

  1. Prepare 2 cup of all purpose flour.
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon of cumin seeds.
  3. You need 2 tablespoons of carom seeds.
  4. Prepare 1 tablespoon of salt.
  5. You need 1/3 cup of heated desi ghee.
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon of curry leaves.
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon of kasoori methi.
  8. Prepare of Sunflower oil to fry.

Salty crunchy snack step by step

  1. First mix all the ingredients and make a hard dough.
  2. Cover and have rest atleast 2 hours minimum 1 hour.
  3. Now in a pan heat up sunflower oil.then turn the flame very low.
  4. Start making flat tortillas of small balls separated from dough and cut in long slices.then twist them and wait one two minutes to hold shapes.
  5. Now check oil must not very much hot.start frying twisted curls of dough and can turn the flame medium.
  6. Make golden and enjoy evening snack homemade.
