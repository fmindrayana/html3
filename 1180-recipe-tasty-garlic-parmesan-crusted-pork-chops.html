Garlic Parmesan Crusted Pork Chopsüòã step by step

  1. Heat 3 Tbsp olive oil in skillet over medium high heat and preheat oven to 350¬∞F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together cheese, bread crumbs, and all seasonings.
  3. Rub the remaining olive oil on pork chops, season with salt & pepper, then dip in the bread crumb mixture, making sure it sticks on all sides.
  4. Place pork chops in skillet and cook for 3 minutes on each side until the outside is crispy and golden brown.
  5. Transfer to a glass baking dish, cook in oven about 20-30 minutes depending on the thickness of your pork chops..
