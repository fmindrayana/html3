Bean Sprout Soup (Instant Pot) instructions

  1. Chop and mince garlic cloves.
  2. Cut white onion into medium sized pieces.
  3. Set instant pot to saute (medium setting), wait until instant pot display indicates "hot".
  4. Add olive oil, garlic, and onion to pot and cook until tender (6 minutes).
  5. Chop celery and add to pot, add spices. Cook for additional 5 minutes..
  6. Make 6 cups won ton soup base to desired flavour..
  7. Add bean sprouts, soup base and stir..
  8. Set instant pot to "soup" setting for 12 minutes. Vent after completion and serve..
