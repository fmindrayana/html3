Instant Pot Beef Stew.

You can have Instant Pot Beef Stew using 14 ingredients and 8 steps. Here is how you achieve it.

Ingredients of Instant Pot Beef Stew

  1. It's 2 lbs of Beef Stew meat, 1-2" chunks.
  2. It's 3 Tbls of flour.
  3. It's 2 Tbls of Extra virgin Olive oil.
  4. It's 1/4 tsp of pepper.
  5. Prepare 1 pkg of McCormick Beef Stew seasoning.
  6. Prepare 4 Cups of Water.
  7. Prepare 4 cups of beef broth.
  8. You need 3 Cups of baby carrots, 16oz bag.
  9. It's 1 of onion, large diced.
  10. It's 1 of large celery rib, diced.
  11. Prepare 3 Tbls of garlic, minced.
  12. You need 6 of medium baby red potatoes, 1 1/2 lbs.
  13. You need 8 oz of sliced mushrooms.
  14. You need 1 Tbls of fresh parsley.

Instant Pot Beef Stew step by step

  1. Toss beef with flour..
  2. Press sauté button, put Olive oil in pot, brown beef on all sides..
  3. Press cancel button. Add stew seasoning packet and water..
  4. Add remaining ingredients except for the parsley..
  5. Place lid on pot, and vent to seal..
  6. Press Meat/Stew button, and adjust pressure to high, and 30-35 minutes..
  7. When pot beeps at finish, allow to natural release until the pin drops..
  8. Top with fresh parsley, Serve with fresh bread. Enjoy!.
