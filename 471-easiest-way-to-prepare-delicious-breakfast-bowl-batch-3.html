Breakfast Bowl Batch 3.

You can cook Breakfast Bowl Batch 3 using 15 ingredients and 6 steps. Here is how you cook it.

Ingredients of Breakfast Bowl Batch 3

  1. It's of Eggs.
  2. You need 6 of large eggs.
  3. You need 1/3 teaspoon of salt.
  4. Prepare 1/3 teaspoon of ground black pepper.
  5. You need 3 tablespoons of butter.
  6. You need of Potatoes.
  7. It's 1-1/2 pound of red potatoes.
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup of crispy smoked bacon.
  9. It's 1/2 cup of fried smoked ham.
  10. It's 1/2 of small onion.
  11. It's 1/3 cup of water to steam.
  12. It's 1 cup of gouda cheese chunks.
  13. It's 1 teaspoon of salt.
  14. You need 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper.
  15. Prepare 3 tablespoons of chopped chives.

Breakfast Bowl Batch 3 instructions

  1. Fry the bacon get it crispy, and set aside, and save the drippings. Fry the ham in the bacon drippings. The ham will render also. When the ham is done set aside and save the drippings..
  2. Wash the potatoes and slice to fry German style potatoes. Add the slices to the drippings and coat all the potatoes and fry..
  3. Dice the onion add along with the spices. Fry turning every so often. Cut the cheese..
  4. Start breaking the cheese into small chunks. Chop the bacon and ham. Keep frying and stirring the potatoes till fork tender..
  5. Add the bacon, ham, and cheese. Add water cover and allow the cheese to steam melt..
  6. Add the chives to the top of the potatoes. In another pan melt the butter for frying the eggs. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper. Fry in the skill till just done. Serve both in a bowl. I hope you enjoy!!!.
