Deep Fried Pork Belly With BBQ Sauce instructions

  1. Best remove the skin, Marinated the pork belly for 1 hour with the bbq sauce and water.
  2. Drip dry then deep fry it for about 8 minutes then slice it up thinly into easy bite size.
  3. Pan fry it for another 4 to 5 minutes together with some raw garlic.
  4. Deglaze the pan with the marinated sauce and bring it to a boil.
  5. Enjoy it as it is, I enjoy this with my Apple and basil With Caramelised balsamic vinaigrette.
