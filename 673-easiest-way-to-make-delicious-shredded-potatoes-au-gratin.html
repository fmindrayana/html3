Ingredients of Shredded potatoes Au gratin

  3. You need 4 cup of almond milk.
  4. It's 1 tbsp of worcestershire sauce.
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp of liquid smoke.
  6. You need 1 1/2 tsp of garlic powder.
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  8. You need 1 tsp of chili powder.
  9. You need 1 of few shakes Tabasco sauce.
  10. Prepare 1 of salt and pepper to suit your taste.
  11. You need 1 cup of sour cream.
  12. You need 3 cup of grated sharp cheddar cheese.
  13. Prepare 1 of onion grated.
  14. It's 6 large of baking potatoes shredded.
  15. It's 1 1/2 cup of grated Colby Jack cheese.
