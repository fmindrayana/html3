Ingredients of Enoki wrapped in beef

  1. Prepare 1 packet of enoki.
  2. It's 1 of tray of beef shabu.
  3. It's 1 clove of grated ginger.
  4. You need 1 clove of grated shallot.
  5. Prepare 1 clove of minced garlic.
  6. It's 3 tablespoon of sake.
  7. It's 4 tablespoon of soya sauce.
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon of sugar.
  9. Prepare 1 portion of scallion.

Enoki wrapped in beef step by step

  1. Wrap each enoki bunch with 2 pieces of shabu beef.
  2. Prepare sauce: Put shallot, ginger, garlic in one mixing bowl. Add sake, soya sauce, sugar.
  3. Heat up pan and pour some oil. Pan fry the rolled beef with the roll ends at the bottom to fry first. Slowly rotate the rolls to make sure all sides are cooked..
  4. After juice from the meat and enoki oozes out, and the meats are cooked, pour in the sauce to cook a little more. Add scallion. Ready to serve..
