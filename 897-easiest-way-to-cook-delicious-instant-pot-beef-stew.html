Ingredients of Instant Pot Beef Stew

  1. It's 1 pound of chuck stew beef, small cubes.
  2. Prepare 2 Tbs of flour.
  3. You need 1/2 pound of carrots, peeled and sliced.
  4. You need 1 of medium white onion, diced.
  5. Prepare 1 pound of white or golden potatoes, cubed.
  6. It's 6 cloves of garlic, minced.
  7. Prepare 4 cups of low sodium beef stock.
  8. Prepare 3 Tbsp of steak sauce.
  9. It's 2 tsp of thyme, rosemary.
  10. It's to taste of S&P.
  11. It's 1 bag of frozen peas, thawed.

Instant Pot Beef Stew step by step

  1. Put IP on sautè mode, add 2 Tbs olive oil.
  2. Add beef.
  3. Cook until about half done, add flour and continue cooking.
  4. Add veggies (except peas), stock, sauce and seasoning.
  5. Put IP on stew setting and set time for 35 minutes.
  6. Release steam and open IP, add peas.
  7. Serve with crusty bread or hearty crackers or use my biscuit recipe.
