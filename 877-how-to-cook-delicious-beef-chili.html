Ingredients of Beef Chili

  1. You need 2 tablespoons of oil.
  2. It's 3 of medium onions chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 of red bell pepper chopped.
  4. You need 1 of small jalapeño chopped (with seeds for hotter flavor).
  5. Prepare 8 of garlic cloves chopped.
  6. It's 1 of beef bouillon.
  7. It's 2.2 oz of ground beef.
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon of dried onion.
  9. It's 1 tablespoon of paprika.
  10. It's 2 tablespoons of cumin.
  11. You need 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper.
  12. Prepare 15 oz of can of tomato sauce.
  13. You need 15 oz of can of crushed tomatoes.
  14. Prepare to taste of Salt.
  15. You need of To accompany:.
  16. It's of Sour cream or cream cheese.
  17. It's of Shredded cheddar, Monterrey Jack, Asadero and quesadilla cheeses.
  18. You need of Corn tortilla chips.
  19. Prepare of Rice.
  20. It's of Black beans.
  21. You need of Kidney beans.

Beef Chili instructions

  1. Ingredients:.
  2. In a large pan heat the oil in medium temperature Add the onion, the peppers, the garlic and cook it until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the bouillon crushed, mix Integrate the ground beef.
  4. Add the seasonings, cook it for 10 minutes with a semi-covered pan.
  5. Add the tomato cans, cook it with an uncovered pan for 30 minutes, stir it occasionally, taste to adjust the salt.
  6. Beef chili can be eat it alone with shredded cheddar cheese, corn tortilla chips and a tablespoon of sour cream or cream cheese per serving..
  7. Or it can be accompanied with beans and rice. (See recipes here in Rosanas Ideas).
