Ingredients of Hearty Italian Style Beef Stew

  1. It's 1.5 lbs of stewing beef, diced.
  2. It's 3 of celery ribs, diced.
  3. You need 2 of medium/large carrots, diced.
  4. Prepare 1 of large onion, diced.
  5. Prepare 4 of garlic cloves, minced.
  6. You need 230 g of cremini mushrooms, cut into quarters.
  7. Prepare 1 can of cannellini beans, drained.
  8. It's 1 tbsp of tomato paste.
  9. Prepare 2 tsp of Italian seasoning.
  10. Prepare 1 tsp of dried oregano.
  11. It's 2 tsp of dried basil.
  12. It's 2 of dried bay leaves.
  13. It's 1 cup of red wine.
  14. Prepare 1/2 cup of beef stock.
  15. It's 1.5 tbsp of flour or corn starch.
  16. Prepare 1 of large can diced tomatoes.
  17. Prepare of Parsley, chopped (garnish, optional).
  18. It's to taste of Salt and pepper.
  19. Prepare 3 tbsp of olive oil.

Hearty Italian Style Beef Stew instructions

  1. In a dutch oven or pot, heat oil and brown beef..
  2. Once beef has been browned, add onion, garlic, carrots and celery. Cook until onions are translucent..
  3. Add your tomato paste and stir together. Then add your flour or corn starch and your herbs, salt and pepper and stir.
  4. Add your red wine and cook all together until the wine has reduced to about half. Add your canned tomato, and beef stock. Stir and place lid on pot and let simmer on low for about 2 hr or until the beef is super tender and "shreds" when pulled with fork..
  5. In the last 45 min add your mushrooms and your canned beans. Stir and let cook for remaining 45 min. (Remember this is a thick stew, there's not supposed to be a ton of liquid).
