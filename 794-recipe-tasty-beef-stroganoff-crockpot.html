Ingredients of Beef stroganoff, crockpot

  1. Prepare 2 lb of chuck roast cubed.
  2. It's 1 can of cream of mushroom.
  3. You need 1 can of beef broth.
  4. You need 3-4 of garlic cloves minced.
  5. It's 1/2 of red onion diced.
  6. It's 1/4 c of worchestire sauce.
  7. It's of Water.
  8. It's of Salt&pepper.
  9. Prepare 1 pkg of 8 sliced fresh mushrooms.
  10. It's 1 cup of sour cream.
  11. You need 1 package of cooked egg noodles.
  12. Prepare of Parsley.

Beef stroganoff, crockpot step by step

  1. Heat a pan with some olive oil. Start to cook onion and garlic to they soften some. Add meat and seared sides, not cooking through.
  2. Add all ingredients to crock pot stirring well add seasoning to taste...if you feel theres not enough liquid,add more broth or water until it's liquidy but not soupy..
  3. Cook on high 4 hrs. Stir frequently,test meat frequently to check level of toughness. Should be melt in mouth soft by 4 hrs..
  4. Cook egg noodles according to pkg. Keep separate until time to serve......Do not cook noodles in crockpot,they will become too mushy bc they will continue to cook..
  5. Once 4 hrs is up or you are satisfied with the meats tenderness (whichever is first) add the sour cream and mix..
  6. Serve with noodles. Garnish with chopped parsley.
