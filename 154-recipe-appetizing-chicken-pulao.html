Chicken pulao instructions

  2. Heat oil and add onion cook until brown in colour.then then add chicken and cook until color changed..
  3. Add ginger garlic paste.then cook over low flame fr 5 minute..
  4. Add salt and peprica powder and masla cook fr five minute..
  5. Add water.cook untill boiled.
  6. Add rice and cook..
  7. Place over low flame on dm..
