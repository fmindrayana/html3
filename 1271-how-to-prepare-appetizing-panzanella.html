  2. You need 1 of medium red bell pepper, de-seeded and chopped.
  3. You need 1/2 of English cucumber, de-seeded and chopped.
  4. It's 6 of baby tomatoes (I used 2 colour romanellas).

  7. Prepare 1 of shallot, finely chopped.
