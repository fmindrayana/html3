Ingredients of Poorman’s Beef Burrito Bowl

  1. Prepare of Ground Beef.
  2. You need 1 lb of Ground Beef 75/25.
  3. Prepare 1/8 tsp of Salt and Pepper.
  4. It's 1 pkg of taco seasoning.
  5. You need of Rice.
  6. Prepare 1 cup of uncooked long grain white rice.
  7. It's 1/2 tsp of salt.
  8. It's of Black Beans.
  9. Prepare 1 (15 oz.) of cans black beans.
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp of cumin.
  11. It's 1/4 tsp of garlic powder.
  12. It's 1/4 tsp of smoked paprika.
  13. It's of Salsa.
  14. You need 1 of tomato, large.
  15. It's 1 of lime, juice and zested.
  16. You need 1/4 of onion, finely chopped.
  17. You need 1 of jalapeño, minced.
  18. Prepare 1/8 cup of cilantro, finely chopped.
  19. Prepare 1/8 cup of Canned Hot Peppers, diced.
  20. Prepare of Toppings.
  21. You need 6 oz of shredded cheese (your choice).

Poorman’s Beef Burrito Bowl step by step

  1. Add the rice, salt, and 3 cups water to a medium sauce pot. Place a lid on top, turn the heat on to high, and allow the water to come up to a full boil. Once boiling, turn the heat down to low and let it continue to simmer for 15 minutes. After 15 minutes, turn the heat off and let it sit, with the lid in place, for an additional five minutes. Fluff just before serving..
  2. While the rice is cooking, make the beans. Add both cans of black beans (undrained) to a small sauce pot, along with the cumin, and garlic powder. Heat over medium, stirring often, until heated through..
  3. Dice the tomato and hot peppers, mince jalapeño and onions. Mix in a bowl while squeezing lime juice and zest together. Set aside..
  4. In a skillet under medium high heat brown the meat season with salt and pepper. Once browned, reduce heat to low and drain most of the grease. Return to skillet and add taco seasoning with some of the remaining grease and mix in well..
  5. Place rice in bowl, and add toppings. Serve and enjoy!.
