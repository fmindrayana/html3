From both sides of the pond, a Cottage Pie instructions

  1. Preheat oven 400° Fahrenheit.
  2. Microwave the sweet potatoes till done it varies by size so check each one.
  3. Cut beef into manageable bite sized pieces put into a pan add spices, except salt, add celery salt, and cook with a stick of butter.
  4. When the beef is mostly browned add the flour.
  5. Stir in let the raw flour get cooked and coated well on beef..
  6. It will start sticking on bottom its just about done.
  7. Add water stirring constantly.
  8. It will thicken into a very rich gravy.
  9. Add gravy mixture to an oven safe dish.
  10. Cook the green beans with 1/2 stick butter. Cook till tender and caramelized. Then chop up into manageable bite sized pieces..
  11. Put green beans on top of beef gravy mixture.
  12. Add cheese to top of beans.
  13. Take the potatoes out their jackets/skins.
  14. Mash the potatoes and add half a teaspoon salt.
  15. Add to top of cheese.
  16. Spread with a fork, bake in oven 30 minutes..
  17. Let sit 10 minutes serve hope you enjoy! ?.
