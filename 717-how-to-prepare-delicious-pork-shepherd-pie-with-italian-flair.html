  2. It's 1 of onion, sliced or chopped.
  3. Prepare 1 pinch of Italian seasoning.
  4. You need 29 oz of jar of mixed veggies.
  5. You need 2 envelope of mashed potatoes (i used Idahoan baby reds).
  6. Prepare 8 oz of grated cheese (i used Italian blend).
